Welcome to Gearbox to Grandstand

This project is my personal initiative to explain Formula 1 engineering and race strategy in clear, simple language. I break down car technology, upgrades, and pit stop tactics so that every fan can understand the sport, from the garage to the grandstand.

Articles

In this section, I write articles covering the latest F1 technology, car upgrades, and race strategy. My goal is to take complex concepts and present them in a way that is easy to follow for fans of all levels.

Glossary

Here, I define key Formula 1 terms and concepts. This glossary is designed to help fans understand technical jargon and abbreviations used in the sport, so no one feels lost while watching or reading about F1.

About This Project

Gearbox to Grandstand is my personal project to make Formula 1 engineering accessible to everyone. I combine my passion for racing with research and analysis to provide clear, accurate explanations. This project allows me to share my knowledge with fans and build my skills in motorsport media and communication.
